Born in Venezuela in 1991. The youngest of three children of a couple of Agronomic Engineers. My father, a painter who believed in the development of the mind. My mother, a passionate cook who believes in the development of the heart.

I'm a curious person of too many ideas. That motivated me to study multiple creative disciplines until I found product design and fell in love.

I keep an open mind, appreciate the extraordinary in everyday things, and face life with optimism and kindness.

Off the clock, you can find me reading, playing with paint, yarn, or wood, trying a new recipe, burying my face in my dog's fur or trying to beat my boyfriend on Mario Kart.
