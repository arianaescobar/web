Side Project

Surf Club

Date

June 2019

Role

Event organizer, Designer

Team

Ariana Escobar

Javier Arce

Marcelino Llano

Like surfing, but on the internet.

I co-organized a fun event about public web surfing with my friends Javier and Marcelino. We invited three participants to surf the web in front of an audience using their favorite devices, services, and themes.

Together, we designed and made a brand, a website, merch, and even a flag!

Hoverstat.es featured our site for its "playful mouse tracking face with a super smooth wrapping of the text on scroll."
