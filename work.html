Work

As a product designer I harness my intrinsic motivation, strengths, and dedication to my craft to create inclusive and well-rounded experiences that respect and enrich users’ lives.

As a Lead Product Designer at Salesforce, I'm focused on building great developer experiences for millions of developers worldwide.

As a Latina in tech, diversity, inclusion, and belonging are always on my top of mind. Along with my colleagues, I do my best to shape an environment that promotes, sponsors, and attracts a diverse group of professionals with a strong sense of belonging. As a way to give back and help lift others up, I volunteer as a design mentor on ADPList.
