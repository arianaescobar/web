Work

I'm a Lead Product Designer at Salesforce, where I design for one of the largest developer and admin communities in enterprise software. Developer experience has been the thread through my whole career — translating designs into production code, working directly with engineers, and now designing most of my work directly in code using AI-assisted prototyping instead of static Figma mocks. I'm a co-inventor on a pending U.S. patent for a developer metadata inspection tool that started as a vision project and shipped years later.

Accessibility, research, and mentoring have been constants since Carto. Co-founding the Heroku Accessibility Guild, working on their first VPAT audit, pioneering the Jobs-to-be-Done methodology still used internally, and mentoring emerging product designers through ADPList.
